Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 1, 2026

InsTik ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture.
  • Usage Data: We collect information about how you use the app, including download history stored locally on your device.
  • Device Information: Basic device information for app functionality and security purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the InsTik service
  • To manage your account and subscription
  • To process payments through Google Play Billing
  • To improve app performance and user experience
  • To send important notifications about your account

3. Data Storage

Your download history is stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences. We do not store your downloaded videos on our servers. Account information is managed through Firebase Authentication (Google).

4. Third-Party Services

InsTik uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication and analytics
  • Google Play Billing: Subscription management
  • TikWM API: TikTok video processing
  • SnapSave: Instagram content processing

5. Content Downloads

InsTik is designed to download content that you have the right to download. We do not store, access, or transmit the content you download. All downloaded content is saved directly to your device. Users are responsible for ensuring they have permission to download any content.

6. Data Security

We implement security measures including:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for all network communications
  • Screenshot protection to prevent data exposure
  • Root/jailbreak detection for device security
  • Rate limiting to prevent abuse

7. Children's Privacy

InsTik is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt out of analytics collection
  • Cancel your subscription at any time

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.
